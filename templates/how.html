How our whistleblowing service works

Service Commissioners and Regulators expect the highest standards… Employees often feel frightened to raise concerns. Early knowledge lets organisations act confidently and effectively.

By signing up for our service you will join the hundreds of organisations protected by Carewhistle.

What we provide

  • Free onboarding & setup
  • Free branded posters
  • Unlimited report recipients
  • Anonymous password-protected disclosure
  • 24/7 confidential portal
  • ISO 27001-friendly
  • Independent & impartial advisors
  • Strict data protection
  • Toolkit & updates
  • Full relationship management

Process

  1. Reporter enters your company code and submits details
  2. Receives case code & PIN for follow-up
  3. Admin coordinates investigation with a manager

Outcomes

We liaise, compile reports, and ensure accurate documentation. Reporters receive updates once actions are taken.
